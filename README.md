# HR-Analytics-Dashboard-Tableau
Interactive HR Analytics Dashboard in Tableau analyzing attrition trends, workforce demographics, and job satisfaction metrics.

Project Overview

The HR Analytics Dashboard is an interactive Tableau project designed to analyze employee attrition, workforce demographics, and job satisfaction trends.

The dashboard enables HR teams and management to identify high-risk segments, monitor key workforce metrics, and make data-driven decisions to improve employee retention.

Key KPIs 
Total Employees: 1,470            
Attrition Count: 237                  
Attrition Rate: 16.12%              
Active Employees: 1,233             
Average Employee Age: 37

Dashboard Features         
Department-wise Attrition           
Sales department shows highest attrition          
Comparison across HR and R&D       

Age Group Analysis       
25–34 age band shows higher turnover      
Workforce concentrated between 25–44        

Gender-based Attrition        
Slightly higher attrition among male employees    
Age-band comparison for better HR targeting   

Job Satisfaction Heatmap        
Role-wise satisfaction comparison       
Helps identify dissatisfaction-driven attrition risk

Education Field Analysis   
Attrition trends based on education background     
Life Sciences and Medical dominate workforce

Key Insights  
Sales department contributes highest attrition     
Employees aged 25–34 show maximum turnover     
Male attrition slightly exceeds female     
Job satisfaction varies significantly by job role         
Majority workforce lies in mid-career age range
